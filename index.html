    How You Can Consume Responsibly (And Save Money and the Planet Doing It)

    Buying in bulk at package-free shops saves you money in the long run compared to buying single-packaged products. Let's use dried mango as an example.

    Product Quantity Price Cost per Serving
    Trader Joe's Dried Mango 6 oz (4 servings) $4.99 $1.25
    Bulk Dried Mango 2 lbs $27 $1.18

    As you can see, there is not much of a difference in price between the two options. However, the bulk dried mango is a fair trade product, unlike the Trader Joe's dried mango. Plus, what you save on is quality, time, and your impact on the planet.

    It would take 5.35 Trader Joe's bags of dried mangoes to equal 2 pounds. That's 5.35 plastic bags with 5.35 different paths to the ocean. By buying in bulk, you are reducing your plastic waste and contributing to a cleaner planet.
